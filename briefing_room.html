Mission Overview

Welcome to the Briefing Room!🕵

You've been brought in by a small but rising art gallery called Da Vivian Code. Their online presence is growing fast — and so is the number of people visiting their website.

But here's the catch:
The site was originally built by someone who wasn't a professional, and now the gallery owner is getting worried. Could there be security holes hidden under the surface?

Your mission:
Put on your ethical hacker hat and test the security of their server and website. You're going to simulate an attack, just like a real hacker would — but with one big difference:
You're here to help, not to harm. You are a penetration tester now!


Find the weak spots.
Understand how attackers think.
And most of all — help Da Vivian Code protect their art from the shadows of the internet.


This simulation follows the PTES (Penetration Testing Execution Standard) — an internationally recognized framework for professional penetration tests. Before you begin, let's take a quick look at what PTES actually includes — and how it will guide your journey through the Da Vivian Code infrastructure.
